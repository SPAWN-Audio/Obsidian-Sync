> Welcome back to Planning Engineered! Today, we are taking a deep dive into my favorite everyday carry (EDC) pocket notebooks. From classic @field_notes_brand to some incredible alternatives featuring Tomoe River and Kinishi paper, we are covering it all.

Whether you are looking for a rugged pocket companion for quick notes, a commonplace book, or an A6 prayer journal, having the right paper makes all the difference. In this video, I break down the specs, the feel, and the cost per page of the@lochby Notebook, the Dialog Notebook, the classic Field Notes, and the Pocket @bulletjournal I also share a look at some of my favorite EDC pens and give a massive shoutout to Crystal from Licensed to Write for sending over some amazing stationery mail!

If you are a stationary nerd looking for the best pocket notebook to fit your journaling style, this video is for you.
