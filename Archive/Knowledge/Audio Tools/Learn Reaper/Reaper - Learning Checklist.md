# The New DAW "Level Up" Checklist

> [!info] Pulled From Google Gemini

## The Foundation (Setup)
- **MIDI Setup:** Map your **keyboard/controllers** so the DAW actually "hears" your input [^2]
- **Buffer Size:** Set low for recording (less lag), high for mixing (more CPU power) [^3]
- **File Paths:** Point your DAW to your **VST/Plugin folders** and sample libraries [^1]

## The Mechanics (Core Skills)
- **Transport Controls:** Master the shortcuts for **Play, Stop, Record, and Loop** [^4]
- **The Grid:** Learn to toggle **Snap-to-Grid** for perfectly timed edits [^2]
- **The Toolbox:** Find the shortcuts for **Split (Cut), Glue, and Mute** tools [^2]
- **Piano Roll:** Practice drawing notes and using **Quantisation** to fix sloppy timing [^2]

## Creative Flow (Production)
- **Instrument Tracks:** Load a built-in synth and record a 4-bar melody [^2]
- **Audio Clips:** Drag in a drum loop and practice **stretching/warping** it to the project BPM [^2]
- **Automation:** Record a "fade-in" by automating the **Volume Fader** [^1]
- **Presets & Favourites:** Save a "Default Template" with your favourite tracks already loaded to save time later [^4]

## The Finish Line (Mixing & Export)
- **The Mixer Layout:** Identify where to insert **EQ and Compression** on individual tracks [^2]
- **Sends/Busses:** Learn to use a "Return Track" for **Reverb and Delay** to save CPU [^5]
- **Gain Staging:** Ensure your Master fader isn't "clipping" into the red [^6]
- **The Export:** Bounce a **WAV (Lossless)** and an **MP3 (Preview)** of your first loop [^7]
