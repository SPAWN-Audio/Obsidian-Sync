> [!example]- Pseudocode Example
> ### Blinky from Pac-man
> - **Initialisation:** Blinky starts at a specific location on the game board.
> - **Main Loop:** Blinky continuously follows these steps as long as the game is running:
> 	- Targeting Pac-Man: Blinky decides on a specific spot it wants to reach on the game board. This spot is usually where Pac-Man currently is.
> 	- Finding the Path: Blinky figures out the shortest route to get to the chosen spot. It's like planning the quickest way to reach your destination.
> 	- **Moving:** Blinky moves one step at a time along the path it found in step b. It keeps moving until it reaches its destination.
> 	- Checking for Catching Pac-Man: At each step, Blinky checks if it has caught Pac-Man. If it does catch Pac-Man, the game ends.
> 	- **Controlling Speed:** Blinky takes short breaks between moves to control how fast it moves.
> - **End of the Game:** This behaviour continues until the game ends.
