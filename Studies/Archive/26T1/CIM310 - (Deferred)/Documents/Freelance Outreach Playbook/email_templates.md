## How to Use These Templates

Each template is designed to be personalised before sending. The most important rule: fill in every [BRACKET] — especially the first sentence, which should always reference something specific about the recipient.

-   Replace all [BRACKETS] with real content before sending

-   Never send the same first line twice — always personalise the opening

-   Keep subject lines short (under 8 words) and avoid words like 'proposal', 'opportunity', or 'partnership'

-   Send from a custom domain email (name@yourstudio.com.au), never a free email provider

-   Use plain text for cold emails — HTML templates trigger spam filters

-   Send at business hours (9am--11am or 2pm--4pm) for best open rates
