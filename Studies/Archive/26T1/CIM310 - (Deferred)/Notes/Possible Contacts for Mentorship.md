Hi Nick, Thanks for getting in touch, great to hear you’re wanting to make new connections and explore different realms of the sound department. The on-set experience is a good place to start to see how everything fits together, so I suggest you get in touch with the Sound Recordists listed on our website and ask if they have any opportunities where you could arrange to do work experience with them or shadow them for a day or just meet up for a coffee and chat. You could do the same with post-production sound people, ask to take someone out for coffee and ask them questions, then if you gel, maybe consider a mentoring situation. This is quite common. Reach out to everyone on that screenwest page with a listing in the areas you're interested in and see what comes back.
