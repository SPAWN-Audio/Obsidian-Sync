I believe that my experience, combined with my technical and creative skillset and my proactive, positive attitude, aligns closely with Guru’s values and vision, making me a strong addition to your team.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how I could contribute to Guru Productions and will follow up in a week to see if we might arrange a conversation.

Yours sincerely, 

Cody Cork
