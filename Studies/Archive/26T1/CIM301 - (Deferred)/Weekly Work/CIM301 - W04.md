### Activity: Journaling Exercise

> [!info] #### Journaling Info
> - Journaling can be thought of as "writing as thinking" or "thinking by writing"
> - Journaling is a very useful tool for ensuring emotional awareness & clarity, professional and personal growth, and logging information.
> - There is great value in externalising our ideas about things going on in our lives, how we feel, what our plans are, any issues we're having, etc.
> - Journaling can help identify personal challenges, achievements, and areas for improvement, which is essential for ongoing development.
> - The type of journaling we'll be doing for this project centres more around professional development based on observations and experience: what did I do, what did I learn while doing this, how might I use this new knowledge in the future?

> [!example] #### Journaling Exercise
> For the next 20 minutes or so, explore each of these reflective prompts, looking at a range of
> topics/considerations. Feel free to do this by hand or digitally.
> 
> ##### Consider a creative project you've recently undertaken, or a particular important studio session. Use either of the following prompts to unpack the session:
> 
> - What worked?
> - What didn't work?
> - What could be improved on moving forward?
> 
> - What? (What was something interesting that you observed?)
> - So What? (Why was this important?)
> - Now What? (How could this be used moving forward?)
> 
> Write freely for 20 minutes, without worrying about structure or grammar. The goal is simply to reflect. You won't have to share anything you write.
>

### GANTT Chart
 Key components of a GANTT Chart
 
- **Tasks** - The individual steps or activities needed to complete the project
- **Timeline** - Dates or time periods for starting & completing tasks
- **Dependencies** - Relationships between tasks *(Task B can only start after Task A is completed)*
- **Milestones** - Significant points or achievements within the project

> [!example] #### Activity 
> ##### Spend 10 mins now brainstorming all the key tasks & steps needed to fulfil the project
> - These may be drawn from your deliberate practise plan you worked on last week
> ##### Organise these into some appropriate categories such as
> - **Research** - Learning techniques, tools or materials
> - **Practise / Development** - Time spent actively practicing or creating. these sessions should be done very regularly
> - **Feedback & Review** - Getting feedback on progress
> - **Final Deliverables** - Completing the project & preparing for evaluation or sharing 

Carry out the process of building a GANTT Chart over [Free Online Gantt Chart Software](https://www.onlinegantt.com/#/gantt)
