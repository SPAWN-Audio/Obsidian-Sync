# Overview

Creative media practitioners need to continually develop and refine their skills in order to remain employable, adaptable, and creatively engaged. This project is designed to help you take ownership of that process by identifying a specific area of your practice you wish to improve, and designing a structured plan to develop that skill through deliberate, focused practice.

In this project, you will analyse the demands of your chosen area of practice by engaging with current industry examples and practitioner approaches, and reflect on your own existing skill set in relation to these expectations. 

Drawing on principles of goal setting and deliberate practice, you will then design a Creative Skill Development Plan that outlines what you intend to practice, how you will practice it, and why this approach is appropriate for your goals. The emphasis is on iteration, refinement, and learning through doing, rather than producing a single polished outcome

You will create a proof of concept asset that demonstrates how your deliberate practice approach will translate into practical outcomes. This proof of concept functions as an initial test of your plan and forms the foundation for Project 2, where you will carry out the full practice process. 

By the end of this project, you should have a clear, realistic, and industry- informed template for developing a specific creative skill through sustained practice in the future.


> [!example] ## Project Goals
> To develop a deliberate practice plan for improving a skill, technique or knowledge area of your choice, relevant to your creative media practice and career aspirations. Your project must centre around practice rather than theory: you must produce a collection/portfolio of creative work that demonstrates the application and development of a new skill or technique, or the application of new knowledge within your creative practice.
