## Notes
1. Create little goals - things you can work on & complete each & everyday. Get the *momentum* going. 
	- *Even small tasks, just attach them to small achievable goals* 

![[Goals - Arnold Blueprint 1.png|306x264]]


2. Instead of *Zoom In* from a broader place, complete & get a rhythm with the smaller everyday tasks, then create weekly & monthly goals. 
	- *Build out upon your system - starting from a complex position can make things harder to kick things off.*
	- Instead of writing a vague resolution - start with simply ***3x daily goals***
