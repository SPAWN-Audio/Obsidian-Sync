## The What
> SMARTER goals are a framework for setting clear and achievable objectives. The acronym stands for:

1. **Specific:**
2. **Measurable:**
3. **Achievable:**
4. **Relevant:**
5. **Time Bound:**
6. **Evaluated:**
7. **Revised:**

Using SMARTER goals helps ensure that objectives are clear, realistic, and trackable.

The framework should be used as a checklist to ensure the goal statement is as clear and
achievable as possible.
