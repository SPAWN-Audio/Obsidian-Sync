# Habit Tracking Outline | ChatGPT

This was initially pulled from a ChatGPT conversation.

## Foundational Daily Habits

_(Keep them small and achievable to build streaks early)_

- **Hydration baseline** — e.g., 2–3L water per day, or a set “glass first thing in the morning.”
- **2–5 minute stretch** after waking (prevents stiffness and wakes up the body).
- **One mindful breath** before starting work (simple grounding habit).
- **Daily sunlight** for at least 10 minutes (morning is best for circadian rhythm).
- **One “win” task** done before lunch (helps productivity momentum).

---

## Health & Energy

- **Short walk** after a meal (great for digestion + glucose control).
- **2-minute plank** or push-up set (easy entry to strength work).
- **Sleep routine anchor** — same bedtime or wind-down ritual daily.
- **Track caffeine cut-off** — e.g., no coffee after 2 pm.

---

## Learning & Creativity

- **Read 2 pages** (books, articles, or professional resources).
- **Learn 1 new thing** in your field or hobby (micro skill acquisition).
- **Daily journal snippet** — 3 bullet points about your day or thoughts.
- **30 seconds of idea capture** — voice memo or note for any random ideas.

---

## Social & Mindset

- **Send one message** to a friend, collaborator, or mentor.
- **Note 1 thing you’re grateful for** (same time each day).
- **Compliment or thank someone** intentionally.

---

## Creative Audio/Engineering Habits (since you’re in audio)

- **5 minutes of sound exploration** — record, layer, or tweak something.
- **Catalog 1 sound/sample** into your library daily.
- **Listen to a track with intent** — focus on mixing choices or design elements.

---

## Pro Tips for Habit Trackers

- **Start with 3–5 habits max** — early overwhelm kills momentum.
- **Pair habits** — attach a new one to something you already do (e.g., “after brushing teeth, do stretches”).
- **Track “streak days” not perfection** — missing once is fine, missing twice is the danger.
