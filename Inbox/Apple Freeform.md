# Resources



# What is Freeform?

> **Apple's free, infinite canvas whiteboard app**
> 
> - *Drop in anything: text, photos, sketches, sticky notes, links, shapes, PDFs*
> - *No rigid structure — flexible, open canvas*
> - *Available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac*
> - *Real-time collaboration/sharing built in*
> - *Completely free, no subscription — comes pre-installed on Apple devices*

# Use Cases

### Brainstorming
- Video ideas, product concepts, course outlines
- Visual thinking without being confined to a document structure
### Digital Scrap Paper
- Quick calculations, measurements, phone call notes
- More flexible than Notes app, and easily expandable
### Meal Planning
- Drops in recipe photos, saved Reels / TikToks, organises by day of the week
- Richer and more visual than a list-based approach
### Home Projects
- Saves Pinterest inspiration, paint colours, furniture dimensions
- DIY project reels all in one visual space
- Uses the **Scenes feature** to quickly jump between rooms without scrolling/zooming around the canvas
