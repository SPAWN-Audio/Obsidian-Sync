# Project Notes

> [!example] Logline
> A grieving woman is haunted by an entity that wears her wife’s face, feeding on her sorrow in a twisted analog-horror romance

## 3x Characters

- **Mina** - Main, desperate to fill the warmth of her dead wife again
- **Juni** - Mina's dead wife, _was_ full of energy & life, though never had _red eyes..._
- **The Entity** - Shapeshifting Cosmic Horror, impersonating Juni. Acts almost tauntingly, lots of analogue horror vibes

## Themes & Aesthetic

| RELEVANCE                                                                                       | NOVELTY                                                                                          |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Grief and loneliness are shaped by the digital age&#xA;                                         | Combines queer romance with analog horror in a unique way                                        |
| Young people are constantly exposed to memories through photos, videos, and social media        | Reinterprets horror tropes (corrupted tapes, uncanny doubles) through grief and love             |
| ==Technology preserves the past in ways that are both comforting and painful==                  | Uses 2D animation techniques (VHS flicker, loops, distortions) as metaphors for memory and decay |
| Mina replaying Juni’s videos reflects real-world digital mourning habits                        | ==Blends tenderness with fear to reshape the emotional tone of horror==                          |
| ==Digital platforms can trap people in a loop of remembrance that’s difficult to move on from== | Centers a queer love story to expand and evolve the genre into new territory                     |

- _A blend of **comforting** & **painful** - **tenderness** & **fear**_

| AESTHETIC                 | DESCRIPTION                                                                                     |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **STYLE**                 | Analog horror with a liminal, dreamlike aesthetic                                               |
| **LIGHTING & ATMOSPHERE** | VHS textures, glitch overlays, and static noise to convey distortion without extra animation    |
| **SOUND DESIGN**          | Distorted audio, static, and dreamlike ambience to heighten tension and reduce visual workload. |
| **FOCUS**                 | Emotional impact over complex animation — horror built through lighting, sound, and atmosphere  |

- _Think lots of old school analogue aesthetics - VHS, Tape, Static, Fuzz, etc_
