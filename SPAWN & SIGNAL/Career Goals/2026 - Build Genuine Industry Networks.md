# Brief 

> [!example] #### Goal 2 - 🌐 Build Genuine Industry Networks
> - By December 2026, I will make at least 20 meaningful industry connections — through online communities, Discord servers, LinkedIn, and local events — engaging consistently enough that at least 5 people know my name and what I do.

- **ADHD adjustment:** Networking as a single big goal is overwhelming and vague. Breaking it into small, specific social actions makes it manageable and even enjoyable — treat it like collecting interesting people, not cold outreach.
- **Visualise it:** Picture being tagged in a post by a fellow audio pro saying "you should talk to this person" — that's the reputation this goal is building toward.
- **Manageable steps:**
    - Month 1: Join 3 active communities (e.g. Game Audio Discord, Reddit r/gameaudio, r/filmsound, LinkedIn audio groups)
    - Month 2–3: Comment genuinely on 2–3 posts per week — no pitching, just contributing
    - Month 4–6: DM 2 people per month whose work you genuinely admire — ask one specific question, not for a job
    - Month 7–12: Aim for 1 virtual coffee chat per month with someone in film or game audio
- **Potential problem:** Social anxiety or rejection sensitivity (common with ADHD) causing avoidance — reframe every interaction as curiosity, not performance. You're not asking for anything, just connecting.
- **Reward:** After every 5 new connections, do something fun and creative — a passion sound design project with zero commercial pressure.
