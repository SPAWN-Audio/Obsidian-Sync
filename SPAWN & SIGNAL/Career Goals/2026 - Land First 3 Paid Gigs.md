# Brief 

> [!example] #### Goal 1 - 🎬 Build a Professional Portfolio
> - By December 2026, I will create a focused portfolio of at least 5 polished audio projects — minimum 2 film sound pieces and 2 game audio/sound design pieces — hosted on a professional profile (SoundCloud, personal site, or Artstation), ready to send to any client or employer.

- **ADHD adjustment:** Don't wait for perfect projects — done is better than perfect. Each piece just needs to demonstrate one clear skill. Set a "ship it" deadline of 2 weeks per project, not months.
- **Visualise it:** Picture sending a single link to a game studio or indie filmmaker and having everything they need to say yes — right there, polished and professional.
- **Manageable steps:**
    - Month 1–2: Audit existing projects — identify what's usable now vs. what needs finishing
    - Month 3–5: Complete 2 film sound pieces (re-score or sound redesign a short film scene)
    - Month 6–8: Complete 2 game audio pieces (implement sound in a free Unity/Unreal demo scene using Wwise or FMOD)
    - Month 9–10: Build and publish the portfolio page
    - Month 11–12: Refine based on any feedback received
- **Potential problem:** Perfectionism causing stalls — set a hard rule: each project gets 2 weeks of work, then it ships regardless.
- **Reward:** The moment the link goes live, treat yourself to a piece of gear or plugin you've been eyeing.
