## Revolut  
[Revolut | All-in-one finance app for your money | Revolut Australia](https://www.revolut.com/en-AU/)  
* Got an almost instant return on application ~ ***only valid for 14 days (until: 21 Feb ’25)***  
    * ***==$400 UPFRONT FREE==***  
        * This isn’t need to actually *be paid *as it’s taken out of the approved loan  
    * Loan for: $10k over 5 Years | 14.99% PA ~ @ $247/M  
    * Is roughly about ~ $290/M when reduced to 4 years  
  
  
  
## Plenti  
[Personal Loans | Unlock a Faster, Fairer Rate with Plenti](https://www.plenti.com.au/personal-loans/)  
* With the same details as the Revolut application. *($10k over 4 Years | $290/M Repayments)*  
* The custom Interest Rate seems to end up around the same *(if not slightly higher):*  
    * 15.5% PA - 16.3% PA ~ Plenti  
        * ***14.99% PA ~ Revolut***
