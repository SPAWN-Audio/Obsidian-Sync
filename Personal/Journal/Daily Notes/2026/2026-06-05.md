## Product vs Project Management

> *Saw a reddit post talking about both & realised I have no idea what **really** differentiates the two*

### Product Management
Product management focuses on the **"what" and "why"** of a product, steering its long-term vision and market success. 
### Project Management 
Project management focuses on the **"how" and "when"**, shepherding temporary tasks to completion within scope, budget, and time constraints
