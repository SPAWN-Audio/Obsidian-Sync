# Brief

> [!example] #### Goal 2 - 🏠 Get out of the House Regularly
> - By December 2026, I will leave the house for a non-essential reason at least 2 times per week — starting with solo, low-pressure outings (a walk, a café, a library) and building toward interest-based group activities by mid-year.

- **ADHD adjustment:** "Be more social" is too big and vague. This goal starts with simply being _outside and among people_ — no interaction required. Exposure to the world in small doses rebuilds tolerance without triggering overwhelm.
- **Visualise it:** Picture yourself sitting in a café with headphones in, working on audio — technically alone, but _out in the world_. That counts. That's the first step toward being comfortable in shared spaces again.
- **Manageable steps:**
    - Month 1–2: Two solo outings per week — walk, coffee, drive, anything outside the house
    - Month 3–4: Find one interest-based group or recurring event (e.g. audio meetup, record fair, gaming night, film club)
    - Month 5–6: Attend that event at least once — no expectations, just observe and exist there
    - Month 7–12: Make it a monthly regular, building familiarity with the same people over time
- **Potential problem:** Anxiety spiking before leaving — create a simple "exit ritual" (keys, headphones, one deep breath) that signals to your brain this is safe and routine, not a big deal.
- **Reward:** After every full week of 2+ outings, give yourself a genuine guilt-free rest day — full hermit mode, earned and intentional.
