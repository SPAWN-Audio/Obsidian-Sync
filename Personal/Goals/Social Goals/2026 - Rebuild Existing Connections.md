# Brief

> [!example] #### Goal 1 - 🤝 Rebuild Existing Connections 
> - By December 2026, I will consistently reach out to at least 1 person I already know (friend or family) once per week — whether a text, voice note, meme, or a call — aiming to have at least 1 face-to-face catch-up per month by the second half of the year.

- **ADHD adjustment:** Reaching out to strangers is exhausting — start where trust already exists. A meme or voice note counts. The act of reaching out is the win, not the depth of the conversation.
- **Visualise it:** Picture sitting across from someone who genuinely knows you, laughing about something familiar — that ease and warmth that only comes from people who already get you.
- **Manageable steps:**
    - Month 1–2: Write a list of 5–10 people you've drifted from but genuinely like. That's your pool
    - Month 3–4: One low-effort message per week — no pressure for it to lead anywhere
    - Month 5–6: Suggest one casual, low-stakes meet-up (coffee, a walk, watching something together)
    - Month 7–12: Aim for at least 1 in-person catch-up per month
- **Potential problem:** Fear of awkwardness after a long gap — most people are just happy to hear from you. A simple "hey, been thinking about you lately" is always enough to restart.
- **Reward:** After your first in-person catch-up, do something you genuinely enjoy that evening — pair the social effort with something comforting to close the loop positively.
