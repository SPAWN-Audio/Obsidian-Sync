> - By Month 6, I will have a clear, written debt repayment plan in place — prioritised correctly, with realistic targets based on actual income at that point — and will have made at least one proactive contact with a creditor or debt support service to begin formally addressing the situation.
> - [[2026 - Create Debt Repayment Plan]]
