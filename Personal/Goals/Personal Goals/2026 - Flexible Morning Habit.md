# Brief

> [!example] #### Goal 1 - ⛅ Flexible Morning Habit
> - By December 2026, I will follow a simple 3-step morning routine (wake up, drink water, write down 1 intention for the day) at least 4 days per week, tracked with a visible habit app or physical checklist on my wall.

- **ADHD adjustment:** Only 3 steps — no overwhelm. The bar is low enough to win on hard days, which builds momentum.
- **Visualise it:** Picture starting the day with _one clear focus_ instead of spinning between tasks — that feeling of direction before the noise kicks in.
- **Manageable steps:** Week 1 is just waking up at the same time. Add water in Week 2. Add the intention in Week 3. Don't rush it.
- **Potential problem:** Forgetting entirely — place your checklist physically somewhere you _cannot miss_ it (bathroom mirror, phone lock screen).
- **Reward:** Give yourself a small weekly treat (coffee, show, guilt-free downtime) every week you hit 4+ days.
