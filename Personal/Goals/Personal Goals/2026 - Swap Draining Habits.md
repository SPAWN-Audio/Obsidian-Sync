# Brief

> [!example] #### Goal 3 - 💫 Swap Out Draining Habits
> - By December 2026, I will replace one identified draining habit (e.g. doom-scrolling before bed) with one enjoyable, low-effort alternative (e.g. 10 minutes of a podcast or audiobook), practised at least 3 nights per week — reviewed every 4 weeks to see what's working.

- **ADHD adjustment:** The replacement habit must be _genuinely enjoyable_, not just "good for you." ADHD brains need interest and reward now, not later. 3 nights a week is realistic without triggering shame on the other nights.
- **Visualise it:** Picture lying in bed feeling _calm and stimulated_ in a good way — winding down with something you actually like, instead of a phone spiral that leaves you wired at 1am.
- **Manageable steps:** Spend the first 2 weeks just _noticing_ the draining habit — no pressure to change yet. Awareness first.
- **Potential problem:** Motivation crashing after the novelty wears off — keep a rotating list of 3–4 replacement options so you can switch when boredom hits.
- **Reward:** After 3 months of consistent effort, treat yourself to something meaningful — a new book, experience, or item you've been wanting.
