# Brief 

> [!example] #### Goal 2 - 🪴 Minimal Planning System
> - By December 2026, I will spend 10–15 minutes every Sunday identifying just 3 priorities for the week ahead, using whatever tool feels easiest (sticky notes, phone notes, whiteboard), for at least 40 out of 52 weeks.

- **ADHD adjustment:** Traditional planning systems are too long and rigid for ADHD brains. This is intentionally short, flexible, and forgiving — 40 weeks leaves room for the inevitable off weeks without guilt.
- **Visualise it:** Picture Sunday evening feeling _settled_, not anxious — knowing your top 3 things and letting go of everything else.
- **Manageable steps:** For the first month, just write the 3 priorities — no reviewing, no scheduling. Add a quick 5-minute past-week reflection only once that feels easy.
- **Potential problem:** The system feeling boring and getting abandoned — switch tools freely (sticky note one week, phone the next). The _habit_ matters, not the tool.
- **Reward:** At the end of each month, count your wins from your weekly lists — seeing concrete progress is deeply motivating.
